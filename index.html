Massage Techniques in Shampooing

This infographic covers all 5 massage techniques used in hairdressing.

Effleurage

Light gliding movements to relax and spread product.

Petrissage

Kneading movements to stimulate circulation.

Friction

Circular rubbing to cleanse and lift residue.

Tapotement

Light tapping for stimulation (used carefully).

Rotary

Large circular movements using palms to stimulate scalp gently.

Key Rule: Always adapt technique to scalp condition.
